# JCP HEP School
Welcome to the JCP HEP School Repository! <br/>
The JCP HEP School is a monthly workshop organized by graduate students in high-energy phenomenology in South Korea. We conduct lectures on the background knowledge, standard tools, and current status of our respective research fields. <br/><br/>
